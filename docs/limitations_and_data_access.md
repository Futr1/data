# Limitations And Data Access

## Public Release Limitations

This repository does not publish:

- raw clinical radiographs
- raw structured EHR
- patient-level split identifiers
- full institutional review materials

## Why These Assets Are Withheld

- patient privacy
- ethics and institutional governance
- cross-center data-sharing constraints
- re-identification risk in small clinical subgroups

## What Is Publicly Released

- code wrappers
- configuration files
- sample schemas
- split summaries
- de-identified examples
- evaluation utilities

## Suggested Data Availability Statement

> Code, model configuration files, split definitions, de-identified metadata schemas, and evaluation scripts are available in this repository. Owing to patient privacy, institutional governance, and ethics restrictions, raw radiographs and structured electronic health records are not publicly released. Access to controlled study materials may be considered upon reasonable request and subject to institutional approval.
